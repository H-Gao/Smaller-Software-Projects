Vision Statement #1: Individual

 

Thevision statement will be used as a guide for your Canadian Heritage FairPavilion. Individually, students should answer the following questions on aseparate piece of paper.

 

a)    Listor describe the main ideas, themes or categories for your topic (ie.  5 W + H)

b)   Whatdo you envision for your 3 –D model?  (ie. what will youbuild, what will you create, artefact) Draw a diagram.

c)    Whatdo you envision for your interactive element? (ie.  what will the judgesor guest do? )  Explain in detail.

d)   Howwill you present your information?  Bespecific.   (keep in mind that itwill be loud, crowded etc.)

e)    Whatwill make your booth memorable? Explain.

f)     Make atimeline for completion of steps.

 Vision Statement#2:  Group

 

Thegroup vision should be made by sharing and building on each member’sindividual vision statement.  Groupsshould spend time on the vision statement to ensure that project is wellorganized and creative. As a group, answer the following questions on aseparate piece of paper.

 

 

1.    Whatwill be the theme of your booth?  (ie.  Hospital, time machine, talk show etc.)

2.    Whatwill be your 3 –D model?  Draw a diagram and explain.

3.    Whatwill be your interactive activity? Explain in detail.

4.    Howwill you set up the poster board?  Howwill this be original?

5.    Whatwill you do during the presentation? (approximately 5 – 7 minutes)  Bespecific.

6.    Whatwill make your booth attractive, authentic and memorable?

7.    Wherewould you like to be located in the café? (corner, middle, wall)

8.    Make alist of all the materials that you will need. (ie. Power bar, extension cord).   Keepin mind that materials may cost a lot of money--- be creative!

9.    Allocateresponsibility for each group member. 

10.Createa detailed timeline for completion. Remember you have 3 in class work periods!)
