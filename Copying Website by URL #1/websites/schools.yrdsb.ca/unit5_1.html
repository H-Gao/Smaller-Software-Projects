PEOPLE > TURN OF THE CENTURY > Rene Levesque

What?
• Leader of Parti Quebecois, a separatist party
• Passed the Charter of French language, Bill 101, which placed strong restrictions on the use of English in Quebec
• Promised to make Quebec completely independent from Canada, but failed



Historical Significance
• Under Bill 101, commercial signs had to be in French only
• Limited English education
• Believed that it was necessary for Quebec's immigrants to be French-speakers in order for French culture to survive
• Outraged many English-speaking communities
• As a result, head offices of many English-speaking corporations relocated elsewhere
• Quebec's economy went down
