PEOPLE > TURN OF THE CENTURY > FIRA

What?
• Ensured that any foreign investment in or takeover of Canadian companies resulted in important benefits for Canada, such as job creation or increased exports
• Could block any foreign investment not beneficial to Canada



Historical Significance
• Had little effect on flow of foreign investment (Americans owned 25% of Canadian company shares and stayed the same 10 years later)
• Mulroney government scrapped FIRA and replaced it with Investment Canada
• Role more limited -> reviewed takeovers only if company's selling price exceeded $5 million
• Welcomed American investment
• Increased Canada's economy, and American influence on Canada
