EVENTS > TURN OF THE CENTURY > Triple Alliance

What?
• alliance made up by Germany and its allies (including Austria-Hungary and Italy)
• also known as the "Central Powers"



Historical Significance
• these alliances were supposed to keep peace in Europe, but ironically, it helped to trigger WW1
• this is because war with any allied nation meant war with the whole alliance, making it dangerous if war were to break out anywhere in Europe
