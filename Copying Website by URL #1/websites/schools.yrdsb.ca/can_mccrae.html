Historical Significance
• poem gave people back then (who weren't involved with the war) and even now, what it really felt like to be fighting
• people realized that fighting in a war wasn't all that glamorous as it was made out to be
• poem is now read every Remembrance Day in memory of the soldiers lost in battle
