PEOPLE & EVENTS > WORLD AT WAR > John Diefenbaker

What?
• 17th Prime Minister of Canada (1957-1963), leader of Conservatives
• elected with greatest majority in Parliament in Canadian history
• against the use of nuclear warheads
• brought into is government many people of varied races: Chinese, Ukranian, Aboriginal, also responsible for naming 1st woman to federal Cabinet


Historical Significance
• introduced Bill of Rights in 1960 to reject discrimination of race, national origin, gender
• made decision to scrap Avro Arrow
• refused to arm Bomarc missiles with nuclear warheads
• hesitated to put all Canadian forces on alert at United States' request during Cuban Missile Crisis, caused a rift between Canadian & American governments
