PEOPLE > TURN OF THE CENTURY > Preston Manning

What?
• Principal founder of Reform Party of Canada in 1987
• Elected to House of Commons in 1993 for riding of SW Calgary
• Now a Senior Advisor to current Leader of Official Opposition, Stockwell Day



Historical Significance
• Under his leadership, in just ten years, the Reform Party grew from a western-based protest party to become the Official Opposition in Parliament
