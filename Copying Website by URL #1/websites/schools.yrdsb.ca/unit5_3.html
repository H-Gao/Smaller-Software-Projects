PEOPLE > TURN OF THE CENTURY > Marshall McLuhan

What?
• Professor at the University of Toronto
• In 1911, was world's leading theorists of communication
• Created phrases such as "the medium is the message" and "global village"
• Focused on the impact of the computer and technology on humanity"



Historical Significance
• Predicted that the linking of information electronically, would turn the world into a "global village", even before Internet was invented
• Nowadays, the power of Internet technology has made geographic and language barriers disappear
