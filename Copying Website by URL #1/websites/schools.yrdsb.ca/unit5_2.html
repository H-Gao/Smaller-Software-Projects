PEOPLE > TURN OF THE CENTURY > October Crisis

What?
• Oct. 5 1970: 4 men from FLQ abducted British trade commissioner, James Cross at gunpoint
• FLQ wanted Quebec separatism
• Second kidnapping and killing, of Quebec cabinet minister Pierre Laporte followed
• Trudeau brought up War Measures Act



Historical Significance
• War Measures Act took away civil rights of Canadians
• Trudeau argued that invoking the War Measures Act was justified because kidnappings were a widespread conspiracy to overthrow government
• Put a stop to separatist terrorism
• Concern for protecting French language and culture grew and intensified
