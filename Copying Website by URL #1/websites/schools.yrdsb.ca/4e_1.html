PEOPLE > TURN OF THE CENTURY > Bren Girl

What?
• Nickname for female war workers
• Women had jobs in aircraft plants, munitions plants, shipbuilding and contruction.
• Were viewed as a national heroine and appeared on billboards as much as male soldiers



Historical Significance
• Advancement in gender equality
• Women's pays were still less but difference was not as much as before
• A large number took on jobs usually done by men
• The divisions between "women's work" and "men's work" were beginning to blur
