What?
• 10 major treaties forced Aboriginals into small parcels of land called reserves (small parcels of reserved land)
• let immigrants use what was formerly Aboriginal land for their own use
• basis of system was that Aboriginal people would become dependants of federal government
• Ottawa never delivered on promises causing poverty among Aboriginals




Historical Significance
• disrespect for and discrimination against the Aboriginal's left a permanent scar on Canada's history
