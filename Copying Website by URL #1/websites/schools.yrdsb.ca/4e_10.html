What?
• In 1945, the "Big Five" nations (Soviet Untion, US, Britain, France and Canada) decided to attempt to shape a peaceful future for the world
• UN intended to provide a place where nations could meet to find peaceful solutions to world conflicts



Historical Significance
• United Nations and the UN Charter of Rights still helps to keep the peace in North America and around the world
• They help to prevent outbreak of new wars, defend human rights and promote equality among individuals, and encourage friendly relations among nations
