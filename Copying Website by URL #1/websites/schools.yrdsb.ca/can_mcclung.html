PEOPLE > TURN OF THE CENTURY > Nellie McClung

What?
• leading supporter of women's right to vote campaign
• helped form Political Equality League in 1912



Historical Significance
• by making most of her opportunities to speak out publicly, she helped win the Enfranchisement of Women's Act, giving women rights to vote
• eventually all Canadian women won full political rights
