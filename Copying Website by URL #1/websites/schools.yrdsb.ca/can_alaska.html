What?
• a vague boundry between Canada and the United States extending from Alaska
• there was a dispute over who actually owned the territory
• in 1903, 6 officials (3 American, 2 Canadian, and 1 British) joined to settle this once and for all
•British took the side of the Americans
• most of panhandle awarded to Americans




Historical Significance
• Britain's decision left Canadians
• confirmed Canadian suspicions that Britain was willing to put its diplomatic interests befor Canada's
• forced Canadians to become determined to take their nation's destiny into their own hands
